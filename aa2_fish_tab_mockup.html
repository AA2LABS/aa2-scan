Rainbow Trout
Oncorhynchus mykiss · Salmonidae
SAFE TO EAT NATIVE · MT IN SEASON
HABITAT Cold, clear mountain streams and rivers. Yellowstone drainage. Thrives in Montana's Clark Fork and Flathead systems.
EDIBILITY Excellent. Mild, clean flavor. Best pan-fried, smoked, or grilled over open flame within 24 hours of catch.
NUTRITION High Omega-3 (1.9g/100g). 20g protein. Low mercury. Vitamin D, B12, selenium — bioavailable and dense.
REGULATIONS Montana FWP: 5 fish/day limit. Check local water restrictions. License required.
PREP NOTE The Chef: gut and clean immediately. Cold stream water rinse. No need to scale if pan-frying skin-side down in cast iron.
