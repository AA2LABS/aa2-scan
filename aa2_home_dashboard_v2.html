FIRST SCREEN AFTER ONBOARDING

Garmin = electric blue #1BB8FF. Oura = teal #1D9E75. Beats Pro = gold #C49A2A. Oakley Meta = purple #AA44FF. Each device owns its color everywhere in the system.

Each device produces a slow, unique waveform flowing left to right. Garmin is cardiac — sharp peaks. Oura is sleep — smooth sine. Beats is audio-ambient — gentle oscillation. Oakley is neural — micro-tremor pattern.
