BIO BUDDY CLARIFIER — ARCHITECTURE DOCTRINE · PRE-BUILD LOCK
THE TIMESTAMP IS THE KEY
Every clarifier is fired against the exact Garmin and Oura device timestamp. The member's explanation is permanently tied to that moment in time — not an approximation. This is what makes it architecturally accurate.
WHY VIDEO CANNOT DO THIS
A video upload has no reliable device timestamp correlation. The clarifier works because Bio Buddy triggers the question from the hardware event itself. The timestamp match is automatic and exact.
SUPABASE TABLE NEEDED
New table: baseline_clarifications. Fields: member_id, device_timestamp (from biosignal_readings), signal_type, raw_signal_value, member_clarification (text), adjustment_type: "exclude" | "reclassify" | "confirm", created_at.
THREE ADJUSTMENT TYPES
CONFIRM — signal was accurate, counts toward baseline. RECLASSIFY — signal was real but misread (stress was excitement, grief, pride). EXCLUDE — signal was anomalous, remove from baseline calculation entirely.
FEEDS buildPersonalTruth()
Confirmed clarifications update the personalTruth string passed to every scan. After 90 days, the system knows not just James's baseline — it knows his emotional vocabulary. Promotion = excitement. Trail run = positive stress. Anniversary = grief.
OVER 90 DAYS
The membrane accumulates meaning, not just data. By day 90, the Equalizer running a scan for James is not comparing him to a population. It is comparing him to himself — with context no other system has ever captured.
